

Between, Among, Amongst [Beech me]

Between:
-> 2 Objects

Among:
-> More than 2 objects
-> How many? -> Given

Amongst:
-> More than 2 objects
-> How many? -> not Given

For example:
He is between SJ and JS.
I kept the phone between 2 books.
I was among 50 people.
I was amongst the crowd.
SJ is among 4 people.
I was amongst many people.
