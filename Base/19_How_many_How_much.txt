How many and How much

How many and How much, are the members of WH family.

We use how many with countable noun and How much with uncountable noun

How many
For example:
How many kids,
How many pages,
How many pens,
How many days,
How many hairs,
How many stars,
How many roads,
etc...

// We use plural noun with how many.

How much
For example:
How much water,
How much sugar,
How much love,
How much money,
etc...

// We use singular noun with how many as they are uncountable.

Important Points:

If we say with units of uncountable, we use how many with plural unit and singular uncountable noun.

For example:
How many kilograms of sugar?
