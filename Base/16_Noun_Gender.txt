Noun - Gender

There are 3 genders in noun category.
1. Masculine
2. Feminine
3. Neuter

1. Masculine Gender
It denotes a male.

For example:
Boy, Uncle, Brother, Son, Tiger, Actor,
Father, etc...

2. Faminine Gender
It denotes a female.

For example:
Girl, Aunt, Sister, Daughter, Tigeress,
Female Dog, etc...

3. Neuter Gender
It denotes non-living things.

For example:
Book, Pen, Mobile, Laptop, Chair, Table,
Car, Bike, etc...
