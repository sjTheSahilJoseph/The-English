

Along
-> Sath me
-> With person/thing

Alongside
-> Kinare sath me
-> With one end to or towards other end of something

For example:
Will you come along?
You'll get this pen along the mobile.
There's a road along the river.
or
There's a road alongside the river.

// For kinare sath me, we can use both.
