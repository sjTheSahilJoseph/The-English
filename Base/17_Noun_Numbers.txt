Noun Numbers

There are 2 numbers in noun context.
1. Singular Noun
2. Plural Noun

Singular means one.
It denotes one person, one place, one thing, basically one.

For example:
Boy, Rat, Tooth, Man, SJ,
Woman, Girl, Pen, Mobile, etc...

Plural means more than one.
It denotes more than one person, place, thing, basically more than one.

For example:
Rats, Mice, Teeth, Men, Women, Girls,
Pens, etc...
