
Above
Above means "Uper":
1. In List
2. In Abstraction
3. In Level

Below
Below means "Neechy":
1. In List
2. In Abstraction
3. In Level

For example:
My name is above/below his name.
Money is above/below love.
His eyes were above/below water.
My country is above all.
My marks are above his marks.
I scored below 80% in 10th.
His level is below yours.
My name is below SJ's.
