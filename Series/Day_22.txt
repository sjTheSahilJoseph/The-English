Tenses.

NOTE.
Only when the subject do something in a sentence is the sentence of tense.
Otherwise, it's a simple sentence where subject doesn't do anything.
    For Example:
        I play.
        I am playing.
        I will play.

Tenses has 3 times.
    1. Past.
    2. Present.
    3. Future.

Each time has 4 Categories.
    1. Indifinite.
    2. Continuous.
    3. Perfect.
    4. Perfect Continuous.

Present Indifinite.
Identity: (Ta, Te, Te, etc... optionally hai, hain, hon, hun, etc...).
    Affirmative: Subject + Verb 1st Form + (s, es - 3rd person singular) + Object.
    Negative: Subject + do/does + not + Verb 1st form + Object.
    Interrogative: <WH> + do/does + Subject + Verb 1st form + Object?
    Negative Interrogative: <WH> + do/does + Subject + not + Verb 1st form + Object?
    Special Case:
        Interrogative: <WH-Subject> + Verb 1st Form + (s, es - 3rd person singular) + Object?
        Negative Interrogative: <WH-Subject> + do/does + not + Verb 1st Form + Object?

NOTE:
    We use 's', 'es' only in affirmatives.

Where to put s/es?
    When subject is 3rd person singular.
    As hint:
    -> When a word ends with 'o', 'x', 'ss', 'sh', we put 'es'.
        Go -> Goes.
        Mix -> Mixes.
        Miss -> Misses.
        Wish -> Wishes.
    -> When before 'y' there is a vowel, we put 's'.
        Play -> Plays.
        Enjoy -> Enjoys.
    -> When before 'y' there is no vowel, we remove 'y' and put 'ies'.
        Try -> Tries.
        Apply -> Applies.

Practice:
SJ is kursi ko ghasit ta hai.
    SJ drags this chair.
SJ kursi ko nhi ghasit ta hai.
    SJ doesn't drag this chair.
Kia SJ kursi ko ghasit ta hai?
    Does SJ drag the chair?
Kia SJ kursi ko nhi ghasit ta hai?
    Does SJ not drag the chair?
SJ kia ghasit ta hai?
    What does SJ drag?
Kursi ko kon ghasit ta hai?
    Who drags the chair?
Parhai kon nhi krta hai?
    Who does not study?
Parhai kon nhi krte hain?
    Who don't study?
Mai paani me apni ungali dubata hu.
    I dip my finger into the water.
Mai paani me apni ungali nhi dubata hu.
    I don't dip my finger into the water.
Kia mai paani me apni ungali dubata hu?
    Do I dip my finger into the water?
Kia mai paani me apni ungali nhi dubata hu?
    Do I not dip my finger into the water?
    Don't I dip my finger into the water?
Paani me apni ungali kon dubota hai?
    Who dips his finger into the water?
Woh ladka jeetne ki koshish nhi krta.
    That boy doesn't try to win.
App shirt pr bht ziada brush ragarte ho.
    You rub the brush too much on the shirt.
Woh deewar pr kia chipkati hai?
    What does she stick on the wall?
Kon kon khana achy se nhi chabate hain?
    Who all do not chew the food well?
Woh ladke dorne ke baad hafnte kiu hain?
    Why do those boys gasp after running?
Yeh kaam kon krta hai?
    Who does this work?

Present Continuous Tense.
Identity: (Raha hai, rhi hai, rahe hain, rahi hai, etc...).
    Affirmative: Subject + is/am/are + Verb 1st form + ing + Object.
    Negative: Subject + is/am/are + not + Verb 1st form + ing + object.
    Interrogative: <WH> + is/am/are + Subject + Verb 1st form + ing + object?
    Negative Interrogative: <WH> + is/am/are + Subject + not + Verb 1st form + ing + object?
    Special Case:
        Interrogative: <Wh-Subject> + is/am/are + Verb 1st form + ing + Object?
        Negative Interrogative: <Wh-Subject> + is/am/are + not + Verb 1st form + ing + Object?

NOTE:
Is -> 3rd person singular.
Am -> First person singular.
Are -> All Plurals.

Practice:
Pooja masale pees rhi hai.
    Pooja is grinding the spices.
Pooja masale nhi pees rhi hai.
    Pooja is not grinding the spices.
Kia Pooja masale pees rhi hai?
    Is Pooja grinding the spices?
Pooja masale kiun pees rhi hai?
    Why is Pooja grinding the spices?
Pooja masale kahan pees rhi hai?
    Where is Pooja grinding the spices?
Pooja masale kiske sath pees rhi hai?
    With whom is Pooja grinding the spices?
Pooja masale kis larki ke sath pees rhi hai?
    With which girl is Pooja grinding the spices?
Pooja kia pees rhi hai?
    What is Pooja grinding?
Pooja or Archana kia pees rhe hain?
    What are Pooja and Archana grinding?
NOTE: Pooja and Archana is subject. And we treat as single subject. Not two. But the subject is plural.
Woh apni success ka credit kise de raha hai?
    Whom is he giving the credit of his success?
Kis larki ka bhai yahan aa raha hai?
    Which girl's brother is comming here?
    Which girl's brother is comming over here?
Yeh pen kon kharid raha hai?
    Who is buying this pen?
Yeh pen kon kharid rahe hain?
    Who are buying this pen?
Mai aesa kiu soch raha hu?
    Why am I thinking so?
    Why am I thinking like that?
    Why am I thinking like this?
Tum aesa keh kar mera dil dukha rhy ho?
    You're hurting me by saying this.
    You're hurting me by telling this.
Koi mujhe yaad kar raha hai.
    Someone is missing me.

Present Perfect Tense.
Identity: (chuka hai, chuki hai, chuke hain, etc... lia hai, diya hai,,, means subject ne kuch kar diya hai).
    Affirmative: Subject + has/have + Verb 3rd form + Object.
    Negative: Subject + has/have + not + Verb 3rd form + Object.
    Interrogative: <WH> + has/have + Subject + Verb 3rd form + Object?
    Negative Interrogative: <WH> + has/have + Subject + not + Verb 3rd form + Object?
    Special Case:
        Interrogative: <WH-Subject> + has/have + Verb 3rd form + Object?
        Negative Interrogative: <WH-Subject> + has/have + not + Verb 3rd form + Object?

NOTE:
has -> 3rd person singular.
have -> rest all.

NOTE:
For perfect tense, we use verb 3rd form, for past, present, and future.

Practice:
Usne mujhe dhoka diya hai.
    He has cheated on me.
    NOTE:
        With cheat, we use 'on'.
Usne mujhe dhoka nhi diya hai.
    He has not cheated on me.
Kia usne mujhe dhoka diya hai?
    Has he cheated on me?
Kia usne mujhe dhoka nhi diya hai?
    Has he not cheated on me?
Usne mujhe dhoka kiu diya hai?
    Why has he cheated on me?
Usne mujhe dhoka kab diya hai?
    When has he cheated on me?
Mujhe kisne dhoka diya hai?
    Who has cheated on me?
    NOTE:
        Subjct is jo kam krta hai, mai subject nhi hu, I'm the object.
        Subject hai woh.
Hme kis kis ne call kra hai?
    Who all have called us?
Hme kis kis ne call nhi kra hai?
    Who all have not called us?
Mai ne is yudh me, apne logo ko khoya hai.
    I have lost my people in this war.
Apne kyi logo ko gumrah kiya hai.
    You have misled many.
Us aadmi ke bhai ne mujhe kitab di hai.
    That man's brother has given me the book.
    That man's brother has given the book to me.
Apne kon kon si filme nhi dekhi hain?
    Which all movies have you not watched?
